Grid System

Responsive

Responsive classes of different column defines its sizes, offsets, alignments and distributions at different viewports which are xs, sm, md & lg.

Here is an example:

Column1
Column2
Column3
<div class="row"> <div class="col-xs-12 col-sm-8 col-md-6 col-lg-4"> <div class="bg-secondary text-center">Responsive</div> </div> </div>
